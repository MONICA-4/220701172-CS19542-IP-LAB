OPPORTUNITIES


A degree in Computer Science and Engineering opens doors to a diverse array of dynamic and impactful career opportunities. Graduates can delve into software development, crafting innovative applications and systems that drive technological progress. In data science, they analyze vast datasets to extract actionable insights, influencing critical business decisions. The field of cybersecurity offers the chance to safeguard systems and data from evolving threats. With a focus on artificial intelligence and machine learning, professionals can build intelligent systems that learn and adapt.

Cloud computing roles involve designing scalable infrastructure, while web and game development offer creative avenues for building interactive experiences. Additionally, careers in human-computer interaction and networking provide opportunities to enhance user interfaces and manage complex communication systems, respectively. This versatility makes a Computer Science and Engineering degree a gateway to numerous exciting and future-forward career paths.

This degree program prepares students for Advanced Technical Computer Systems Design and Development Work. It includes the study of basics with Data Science, Database Design, Data Communications, Procedural and Object Oriented Programming, Operating Systems Design, Algorithm Development and Applications of Artificial Intelligence. The curriculum includes basic course work in Electronics Technology and the Theory of Programming Languages. Students focus on developing computer-based solutions involving hardware and software components and integration. Graduates are qualified to work in a range of positions, from entry-level computer scientists and system engineers to project managers leading advanced applications of computer science to real-world problems. The department has since broadened its research strengths.
