KERALA

Kerala, located on the southwestern coast of India, is renowned for its lush landscapes and unique cultural heritage. Known as "God's Own Country," it boasts stunning backwaters, serene beaches, and verdant hill stations. The state is celebrated for its diverse traditions, including Kathakali dance and Onam festival. Kerala's economy is significantly supported by tourism, agriculture, and remittances from its overseas workers. It has achieved remarkable progress in health and education, often ranking high in human development indicators. The cuisine is rich and varied, with dishes like appam, puttu, and seafood being popular. Kerala's biodiversity is preserved in its numerous wildlife sanctuaries and national parks.

Kerala’s tourism thrives on its picturesque backwaters, pristine beaches, ands hill stations, attracting visitors with beauty and richness.

Kerala boasts high literacy rates fostering a strong focus on quality education and innovative learning methods.

Kerala offers comprehensive medical services and wellness treatments, emphasizing holistic and preventive care.

The state’s vibrant culture includes traditional arts like Kathakali and Mohiniyattam, celebrated festivals such as Onam, and unique local crafts.
